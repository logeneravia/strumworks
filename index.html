Why it's called Strum

Strike a note, and everything near it answers.

Sand dances on a drumhead. Water trembles in a pail. A sound reaches out, and the world around it responds.

A strum works the same way. Every message opens room for a reply — and a reply is where connection begins. That is what Strum is for: starting a real dialogue between you and the people you serve, one resonant note at a time. You strum; they answer; the relationship grows.
